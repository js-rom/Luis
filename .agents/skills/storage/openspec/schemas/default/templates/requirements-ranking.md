## Ranking Criteria

- **Risk:** Technical complexity, uncertainty, integration difficulty, or business/process ambiguity.
- **Coverage:** How much of the solution space or architecture the requirement touches during early iterations.
- **Criticality:** Business value, compliance impact, or operational urgency.
